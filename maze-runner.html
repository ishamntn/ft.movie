Synopsis

In The Maze Runner, a group of teenagers finds themselves trapped in a mysterious, ever-changing maze with no memory of how they got there. Thomas, the newest arrival, quickly befriends the other inhabitants, who have built a society within the maze, hoping to find a way out. As he uncovers clues about the maze and the organization that created it, Thomas begins to question the purpose behind their imprisonment. The group faces deadly creatures and the threat of being trapped forever, and Thomas takes on the role of leader, determined to find an escape. As they venture into the heart of the maze, they discover shocking truths about the world outside and their roles in a larger experiment. The film explores themes of survival, friendship, and the quest for freedom.
