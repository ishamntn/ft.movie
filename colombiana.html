Synopsis

Colombiana follows the story of Cataleya, a young woman who becomes an assassin after witnessing the brutal murder of her parents in Bogota, Colombia. After escaping the killers, she is taken in by her uncle, a former hitman, who trains her in the art of assassination. Driven by revenge, Cataleya embarks on a mission to hunt down the men responsible for her parents’ deaths. As she carries out dangerous missions for a criminal syndicate, her personal vendetta begins to collide with her work, putting her in the crosshairs of both law enforcement and rival criminals. The film showcases her struggle to balance her pursuit of justice with the danger of her violent lifestyle. Ultimately, Cataleya must confront her past and decide how far she’s willing to go to avenge her family.
